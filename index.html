About Me

I am a senior undergraduate at the University of Maryland majoring in GIS and Geology, and I find that the two fields of study compliment each other nicely. My professional interests are wide-ranging: I enjoy programming interactive web maps with open-source packages and getting out in the field to observe natural processes. It is my goal to find a career that has a healthy balance between earth science and GIS, and I believe my projects reflect that. Feel free to browse through them here.
