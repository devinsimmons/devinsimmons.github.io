About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Maryland with a bachelor's in Geology and a bachelor's in GIS. Currently, I work as a Geospatial Developer Fellow at the US Department of Transportation. Overall, I have a particular interest in analyzing and visualizing spatial data related to urban places and transportation.

In terms of programming and urban geography, I still have a lot to learn. This portfolio has become a timeline of my progress. You can see curated examples of my work here, and find code I've written on my github.
