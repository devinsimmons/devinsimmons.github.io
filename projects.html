Senior Thesis Project: Spatial Analysis of MODIS Evapotranspiration Estimate Rasters Using Python

For my geology degree, I am performing an independent research project under the guidance of Dr. Karen Prestegaard. This study focuses on comparing the MODIS evapotranspiration estimates produced by the MOD16 algorithm with evapotranspiration measurements that were directly measured at flux towers in the Canadian boreal forest.
